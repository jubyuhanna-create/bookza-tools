About CounterPick

What is CounterPick?

CounterPick is a free, community-driven counter-pick and strategy tool built for Overwatch 2 players. Whether you're climbing ranked in Flex Queue, experimenting in Open Queue, or just want to improve your hero knowledge, CounterPick helps you make better decisions in the draft phase.

Features

  • Flex Queue Counter Tool: Pick a map and the enemy team composition to get the best counter-pick, ban suggestions, and perk recommendations.
  • Open Queue Counter Tool: No role lock — pick any 6 enemy heroes and get optimal counters for open queue matches.
  • Hero Patch & Meta: Stay up to date with the latest hero balance changes and patch notes.
  • Tip of the Day: Get a fresh gameplay tip for a random hero every day to expand your hero pool and game knowledge.
  • Strategy Guide: Map-specific team composition recommendations for both Flex Queue and Open Queue, with detailed explanations of why each comp works.

How It Works

CounterPick uses a scoring algorithm that evaluates hero matchups, map advantages, and role synergies to recommend the strongest picks for any situation. All data is based on community knowledge, hero ability interactions, and competitive meta trends.

The entire tool runs in your browser — no accounts, no downloads, no data collection. Just open the page and start drafting.
